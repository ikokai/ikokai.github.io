blizzard of rocks

A space shooter in the arcade tradition for the iPad. Simple intuitive controls and mechanics:

  • Touch and drag: The rocket will follow your finger.
  • Tap anywhere and you can shoot all the nasty asteroids.
  • Collect the yellow stars for bonus points and the blue shields for temporary invincibility.
  • Clear the current field from asteroids, and get to the next sector with more rocks and more fun.
